Kuwait Leave Encashment 2026: Why the Divisor Is 26

→ Open the Kuwait Leave Encashment Calculator

Kuwait's annual leave calculation has one detail that trips people up: the daily-wage divisor is 26, not 30.

The entitlement

You earn 30 days of paid annual leave per year once you complete 1 year of service.

Why divide by 26?

Kuwait's payroll convention treats the working month as 26 days (reflecting a 6-day working week), rather than a flat 30-day calendar month. This is the same divisor used for Kuwait's end-of-service indemnity.

Worked example

KWD 600/month, 20 unused leave days: (600 ÷ 26) × 20 = KWD 462.

Frequently asked questions

How much annual leave do Kuwait employees earn?

30 days per year once you complete 1 year of service, under Kuwait's Labour Law No. 6 of 2010.

Why is Kuwait's leave divisor 26, not 30?

Kuwait's daily-wage convention for leave (and indemnity) divides the monthly wage by 26 to reflect a 6-day working week, rather than a flat calendar-day divisor.

Can I get paid for unused leave while still working?

Employers may agree to encash unused leave during employment; it is also settled as part of your final dues if you leave the company.

Is Kuwait leave pay on basic or full wage?

Full monthly remuneration is generally used, consistent with how the same wage figure is used for Kuwait's end-of-service indemnity.
