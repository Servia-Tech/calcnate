How to Calculate Saudi Maternity Pay

Since the 2025 change, Saudi maternity leave is a flat 12 weeks at full pay. Here is exactly what that comes to, with a worked example.

→ Open the Saudi Maternity Leave Calculator

Saudi maternity pay is now refreshingly simple: 12 weeks (84 days) at 100% of salary, with no tenure tiers. Here is how to size it.

Step 1 — Find your daily wage

Daily wage = Monthly salary ÷ 30

Step 2 — Pay 84 days at full rate

12 weeks is 84 calendar days, all at full pay:

Maternity pay = Daily wage × 84

Because it is a flat full-pay period, this equals roughly 2.8 months' salary (84 ÷ 30).

The sick-newborn extension

If your newborn needs continuous care, add a further one month at full pay (30 × daily wage) plus one month unpaid. At SAR 9,000 that extra paid month is 300 × 30 = SAR 9,000. Enter your salary in the Saudi maternity leave calculator for the exact figure. The leave counts toward your end-of-service service.

Comparing it to the UAE

It is worth seeing the two Gulf systems side by side, because the shape is different even where the goal is the same. Saudi Arabia now pays a flat 84 days at 100% — roughly 2.8 months of salary, all at full rate. The UAE pays 60 days, but only the first 45 are at full pay and the last 15 at half, so the effective full-pay equivalent is about 52.5 days. On identical salaries, the Saudi entitlement is both longer and paid at a higher effective rate. If you are comparing job offers across the two countries, maternity provision is one line where Saudi Arabia currently comes out ahead.

Key takeaways

  • Daily wage = monthly salary ÷ 30.
  • 84 days (12 weeks) all at full pay.
  • That equals roughly 2.8 months of salary.
  • A sick-newborn adds one more full-pay month plus one unpaid.
  • The leave counts as continuous service toward end-of-service.

Frequently asked questions

How much is Saudi maternity pay?

12 weeks (84 days) at full pay. Multiply your daily wage (monthly salary ÷ 30) by 84.

How many months of salary is 12 weeks?

About 2.8 months (84 ÷ 30), all paid at full rate.

Does maternity pay depend on my length of service?

No. Since the 2025 amendment the tenure tiers were removed, so it is full pay regardless of service length.

Is the sick-newborn extension paid?

One further month is fully paid, plus an additional month unpaid, if the newborn needs continuous care.

Is Saudi maternity pay based on basic or full salary?

It is based on your salary during the leave at 100%. Check your contract for the exact components, but the 2025 amendment pays full salary with no tenure test.

Can I take maternity leave before the birth?

Yes. You can begin up to four weeks before the expected delivery date, on a medical certificate, with at least six weeks taken after the birth.

Does the calculator include the sick-newborn month?

You can add it: one further month at full pay (30 × daily wage) plus one unpaid month if your newborn needs continuous care.
